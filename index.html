About NUIIN Delivery

UG EXPRESS DELIVERY is a premium package delivery service operating across Uganda, founded in 2025 with the mission to connect people and businesses through reliable logistics solutions.

Our Story

Founded by Hannah Tumukunde Karema (Miss Uganda 2023-2024) and NUIIN NELYCE, UG EXPRESS DELIVERY was born out of a need for reliable, transparent delivery services in Uganda. What started as a small operation in Kampala has grown into a nationwide network serving thousands of customers monthly.

Our Network

We partner with trusted bus companies, post offices, and independent delivery agents across all regions of Uganda to ensure your packages reach their destination safely and on time.

Our Values

  • Reliability - We deliver what we promise
  • Transparency - Real-time tracking and updates
  • Customer Focus - Your satisfaction is our priority
  • Innovation - Constantly improving our services
